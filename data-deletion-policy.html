Data Deletion Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2025

Last Updated: July 19, 2025

You have full control over your data on Newzora. This document explains how your data is collected and how to delete it.

1. What Data We Store

  • Your profile (name, email, avatar).
  • Your posted content.
  • Your interaction history (likes, comments, bookmarks).

2. How to Request Deletion

To delete your data:

  • Go to your account settings.
  • Click “Delete My Account” to erase all personal data.
  • Or email delete@newzora.com with subject “Delete Account Request”.

3. Processing Time

Your data will be permanently deleted within 7 days of receiving the request.

4. Third-Party Logins

If you signed in via Facebook, deletion of your Newzora account does not delete your Facebook account. You must manage that separately via Facebook settings.

For any concerns, contact us at jxrjqc@hotmail.com
